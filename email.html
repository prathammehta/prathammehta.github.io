Shyne! - Make music﻿

Shyne! is a beautiful and incredibly easy to use music creation app exclusive to the App Store.

We built Shyne! to ensure that anybody regardless of their knowledge about musical theory can just pick up the app and create something stunning.

 

We’ve worked on this project for over 2 years now to come up with the user interface that it uses today. Shyne! has been carefully designed keeping in mind the latest and greatest API. It integrated technologies like 3D touch, Core spotlight and much more. All this allows anyone to start rocking to a beat in a matter of seconds. While testing the application, irrespective of the origin and occupation of the users, there was always a smile on their faces while they created music and sang over it.

The unique idea behind Shyne is that it allows the user to mix music of different genres (Try mixing Indian, Reggae and Techno) and end up with something spectacular. Hence, it’s a great source of inspiration to solo musicians. Rappers have taken quite a lot of interest in Shyne! to help with free styling and hosting rap battles with their friends.

 
Finally, Shyne! can be used with GarageBand and any other application compatible with AudioBus and Inter-app audio. So we don’t restrict the user to our app exclusively. They can choose to integrate our app with the thousands of amazing music apps already in the App Store.

 
We have put our heart and soul into this application for the last couple of year and we think we've come up with something special. We hope that after you try the application, you too will agree with us.
